Fast Aerial Tool

Use this to perfect your fast aerials. For you silvers out there who don't know how to fast aerial, it goes like this.

  • Pull the thumbstick all the way down
  • Jump and hold boost.
  • Release the thumbstick and jump again.
  • Pull the thumbstick down again
Some tips:
  • Hold your first jump for as long as possible
  • Hit your second jump exactly 240 milliseconds after the first. The timing is marked by a chirp and the purple lines.
  • Make sure you start the move with the thumbstick pulled down and try to release it at the last second.
    The faster you lean back, the faster your boost can propell you upward instead of forward
  • If your brain is too smooth to figure out how to work on the thumbstick and the jump button at the same time, just figure out the timing of the jump button first.
